Questions

Everything else
you're wondering.

When does SPOTR launch?

The iOS app is coming soon to the App Store. Early-access athletes and coaches get in first — that's what the waitlist is for. Join now and you'll be in the first wave we onboard.

What does it cost?

SPOTR is free for athletes. Getting recruited shouldn't depend on whether your family can afford a $3,000 recruiting service. Coach and program tools will have paid tiers later, but early-access coaches get in free.

Who is SPOTR for?

Any high school, JUCO, or transfer athlete who wants to play at the next level — every sport, men's and women's. And on the other side: D2, D3, NAIA, and JUCO coaches and recruiting staff who need a direct pipeline to athletes without D1 budgets. D1 staff are welcome too.

How are coaches verified?

Coach accounts are verified against their program before they can message athletes — school email and staff listing. Athletes should never wonder if the person in their DMs is really a coach.

Is SPOTR a recruiting service?

No — that's the point. Recruiting services charge you to blast emails at coaches. SPOTR is a network: your profile and film live where coaches already search, and they contact you. No middleman, no commission, no gatekeeper.

What about my film and my data?

Your film stays yours. You control what's on your profile and who can message you. We don't sell athlete data — the product is the network, not you.
